Electrical Installation Services

It can be tempting to attempt an electrical installation in your home by yourself. You may have a garage full of tools, expert guides or even a tutorial video — but there are upsides to a licensed electrician that just can't be replicated. Look to Toronto Electrical Experts for electrical installations in Toronto homes and benefit by:

savings

Saving Money

We've been doing electrical work since 1989, and our licensed electricians know how to determine the right tools and supplies for any job. We also know the best ways to access the wiring needed for your installation. If the work requires opening walls or other access points, we can be as minimally invasive as possible — so you don't need to spend money on repairs.

gavel

Staying Legal

We stay abreast of all local laws, bylaws and regulations governing electrical work, so you never run the risk of incurring fines or other penalties. Our electricians are fully licensed and insured as part of our promise to always engage in fair business practices. We secure the appropriate permits and work with home inspectors to make sure the work we do is up to code.

schedule

Saving Time

Doing the work yourself means a lot of research and likely more than one trip for supplies and materials — possibly spending more time on preparation than the job itself. There's also a risk of missing something important and needing additional maintenance. Hiring an expert gets the job done as soon as possible, and once we leave you won't need to call us back for corrections.

health_and_safety

Staying Safe

Electricity can be dangerous to both people and property. Our contractors respect the safety process and stay compliant with the Ontario Electrical Safety Code at all times.

Specialized Installation Services

Toronto Electrical Experts has the experts, experience and equipment to handle a variety of installation needs. Consult or hire us when looking to make any of these electrical installations in your Toronto home:

power

Install a Plug

If your appliances are all competing at the outlets or you've found yourself hunting for a spare socket, we can add new plugs to your home to improve power availability.

mode_fan

Install a Ceiling Fan

In addition to being complicated to wire, ceiling fans are typically heavy — it's common for them to weigh over 50 pounds. We can handle the lifting and install with ease.

lightbulb

Install Lighting

The right lights — from wall sconces to pot lights to ceiling lamps — can unlock a room's potential and character. Learn more about our lighting design. Whether indoors for rooms and workspaces or outdoors for path or patio lights, we can do it.

pool

Specialty Installation

Pool lights and pumps, security lighting (especially for motion detectors) and electric heating are all examples of systems that have specialized wiring needs. We can pick the right wire for the job and perform these installations easily.
