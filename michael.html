Michael Mueller, owner and Master Electrician at Toronto Electrical Experts, has a passion for the electrical industry — and it's evident in both the workmanship and the customer service his company provides.

Michael's passion and love for the electrical industry started at a young age, working weekends and summers as an electrician's helper alongside his father, also an electrician. It was clear to him what his career path would be, and so he attended George Brown College in Toronto, where he excelled in the Electrical ICI programme.

Over a five-year span, Michael completed his apprenticeship with companies that gave him exposure to new commercial buildings, residential work and a myriad of other big projects.

In 1992, Michael knew that he could provide the community with superior technical skills and customer service, and so he went out on his own. He is known in the community as an electrician who is skilled, honest and reliable.

format_quote

It is not enough to provide top-notch electrical work. Customers deserve top-notch customer service to go along with that, and it has always been my mission to provide both. I'm proud to say that my loyal customers of many years would agree that I accomplish both, with the help of my team.

Toronto Electrical Experts has enjoyed steady growth. Superior technical expertise, strong customer service and utmost professionalism are the components that make the company stand apart from the competition. As clients often note, "When you meet Michael, it is immediately apparent that his passion for his profession is matched by his expertise."

Michael Mueller holds a Master Electricians License and an Electrical ICI License. He and his family live in North York, Ontario.
